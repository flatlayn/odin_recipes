Taco Skillet


Description

Easy taco skillet bake that uses only one pan. This recipe can be made for under $10!

Ingredients:

  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Diced Tomatoes
  • Water
  • Cooked Rice
  • Taco Seasoning
  • Mexican Cheese Blend
  • Shredded lettuce

Steps:

  1. Heat skillet
  2. Cook and stir beef and onion
  3. Drain
  4. Stir tomatoes, water, rice, and taco seasoning into beef mixture
  5. Reduce heat and simmer until rice is tender.
